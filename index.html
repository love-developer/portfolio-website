What services do you offer as a web developer?

I specialize in creating responsive websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React.js, Next.js, Tailwind CSS, and Bootstrap. I also build custom Shopify and WordPress sites tailored to client needs.

Can you develop Shopify stores with custom features?

Yes, I create fully customized Shopify stores, including dynamic sections, sliders, product pages, and unique features using Liquid, JSON schema, and advanced frontend integrations.

Do you work with WordPress and WooCommerce?

Absolutely. I develop and customize WordPress websites and WooCommerce stores, offering both design and functionality improvements tailored to your business goals.

Are your websites responsive and mobile-friendly?

Yes, all my projects are fully responsive and optimized for all screen sizes, ensuring a pixel-perfect design on desktop and a seamless user experience on mobile and tablets.

Do you offer performance and SEO optimization?

Yes. I follow best practices for website performance, including lazy loading, asset optimization, and technical SEO to ensure fast-loading, search engine–friendly websites.
